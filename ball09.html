.::Welcome to the STM MGOCSM Invitational Basketball Tournament Website!

13 TEAMS, 1 CHAMPION!

CONGRATULATIONS TO THE 2009 MGOCSM TOURNAMENT CHAMPIONS, ST. MARY'S - BRONX!!!
AND TO THE RUNNERS-UP, ST. THOMAS - MASCHER!!!

This year's STM MGOCSM Invitational Basketball Tournament will be held at Kelly Bolish (Renegades) Gym in Hatboro, PA just north of Philadelphia. This is a new facility with three full courts.

There will be a $5 Spectator Admission Fee.

The tournament will be officiated by the Philadelphia Referee Group led by Tony Holmes.

This tournament is open to any Indian Orthodox church. If interested in playing or helping out, please feel free to contact us at: mgocsm.sports@gmail.com

Visit the Facebook Event Page for further details and sign our Guestbook, as both are moderated.
