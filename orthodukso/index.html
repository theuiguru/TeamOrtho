What is Orthodukso?

Orthodukso™ is a collaborative effort of a national network of Orthodox Christian laity. Our mission is to engage American society in a uniquely Orthodox Christian perspective. Through topical and up to date commentary on theology, current events, politics, science, culture or whatever is particularly relevant; we hope to be a point of access for anyone interested in shaping our identity as the Orthodox Church in America.

If you are Orthodox, we hope to bring you with fresh ideas about an Ancient Faith. If you are not Orthodox, we hope to be a valuable resource for understanding the Orthodox viewpoint. For all of our readers, we hope to challenge you to take a second look at what you may have thought was familiar.

Our Five Aims:

  1. To direct inquirers to resources on the theology and spirituality of the Orthodox Church.
  2. To engage the world wide web with the teachings and practices of the Orthodox Christian Church and the Faith handed down from the Apostles.
  3. To provide interesting and informative news and media relevant to a Christian world view.
  4. To facilitate the exchange of ideas on developing an identity for the Orthodox Church in America.
  5. To develop the network of Orthodox Christians concerned about the future of the Church.
